## 💡 Key Insights

### ✅ Healthy Diversification

- Top 5 products contribute only **16.6%** of total revenue  
- Top 5 customers contribute **10.8%**

➡️ Indicates **low dependency risk** and strong diversification

---

### 📉 Purchasing Behavior

- AOV decreased by **6.9%**
- Quantity sold increased  
- Average unit price decreased by **12.4%**

➡️ Drop in revenue per order driven by **lower product pricing**, not demand

---

### 🌍 Geographical Dependence

- Canada & France contribute **47.9% of total revenue**

➡️ Strong markets, but clear opportunity for **expansion into new regions**

---

### ⚠️ Product Dependency Risk

- Bikes category accounts for **78.1% of total sales**

➡️ High reliance on one category → potential business risk

---

### 📅 Weekend Opportunity

- **81.1% of sales occur on weekdays**

➡️ Weekends are underutilized → opportunity for:
- Promotions  
- Campaigns  
- Targeted offers  

---

## 🚀 Business Impact

- Identified **low revenue concentration risk (good diversification)**  
- Highlighted **pricing impact on revenue decline**  
- Exposed **high dependency on Bikes category**  
- Revealed **geographic concentration in 2 key markets**  
- Uncovered **missed sales opportunities during weekends**
